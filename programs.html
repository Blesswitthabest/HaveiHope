What We Do

Programs That Help Children Grow

HaveiHope programs combine learning, mentorship, stewardship, and real-world experiences.

Hope Stewardship

Hands of Stewardship

Hope Stewardship provides children with equine and farm experiences that build discipline, compassion, leadership, responsibility, and confidence.

  • Hands to Build: carpentry, creating, repairing
  • Hands to Care: horsemanship, animal care, compassion
  • Hands to Provide: chickens, eggs, and market stewardship

Hope Harvest

Seeds of Harvest

Hope Harvest teaches children how food grows, why it matters, and how gardening can build patience, responsibility, confidence, and service.

  • Seed of Growth: gardening and plant science
  • Seed of Discovery: STEM learning and observation
  • Seed of Provision: harvesting, food preparation, and generosity

Hope Word

Voice of Word

Hope Word helps children grow in literacy, communication, creativity, and confidence through reading, writing, and mentorship.

  • Voice to Read: vocabulary, comprehension, and confidence
  • Voice to Write: creativity, journaling, and expression
  • Voice to Speak: communication, leadership, and courage
